About the System

Why Blockchain for Health Records?

Traditional health records are fragmented, vulnerable to tampering, and difficult to share. MediCare solves this by anchoring every patient update to the Ethereum blockchain — creating an immutable, transparent, and instantly verifiable record.

Doctors update patient data through a secure portal. Each update generates a new cryptographic hash and QR code. Patients own their data and can share it with any verified doctor in seconds.

Process

How It Works

From registration to blockchain-secured verification in four simple steps.

01
Patient Registers

Admin registers the patient. A blockchain hash and QR code are instantly generated and assigned.

02
Request a Doctor

Patient sends a request to a doctor. The doctor reviews and accepts or rejects the request.

03
Doctor Updates Record

After consultation, the doctor records symptoms, diagnosis, and prescription. A new blockchain hash and QR are generated.

04
Verify Anytime

Scan the QR code to instantly verify the patient's record against the blockchain hash. Tamper detection built in.
